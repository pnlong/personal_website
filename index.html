About Me

Hi! My name is Phillip Long. I'm a first-year undergraduate at the University of California, San Diego (UCSD) studying Computer Science. My research is focused on using machine learning to analyze and generate music. I am part of the McAuley Lab.

In my free time, you can find me jamming with my band, in which I play the trumpet and/or keyboard. I continue to swim and play water polo, though at a much less competitive level than in high school.
